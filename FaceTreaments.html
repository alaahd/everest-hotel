Everest SPA - AMMAN

The Everest SPA at Everest Hotel&Spa - Amman occupies 7000 m2 of space over two floors.The Everest SPA aims to provide the greatest in health and fitness care, providing its clients with certified instructors, experienced trainers and state-of-the-art facilities.

Guests and members can benefit from exclusive access to a modern health club with the best equipment and service. A wide range of group fitness classes and individual training are available, in addition to the latest cardio-vascular machines, wet and dry saunas and superior SPA treatments

SPA facilities include separate men's and women's fitness rooms with breathtaking views of the city, an aerobics studio, sauna, Jacuzzi, juice bar, indoor and outdoor pool (during the warm summer months), a mudroom, ladies' hairdressing salon and barber's shop.

Guests below 16 years old are not allowed to use the indoor swimming pool & spa area (and they have an access to the outdoor pool)
